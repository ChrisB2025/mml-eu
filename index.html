Frequently asked questions

Find clear answers to common questions about our mission, events, and Modern Monetary Theory.

  • What is Modern Monetary Theory?

    Modern Monetary Theory (MMT) is a macroeconomic framework that explains how sovereign governments with their own currency manage fiscal and monetary policy. It highlights the mechanics of money creation, public spending, and taxation.

  • Who can benefit from your events?

    Our events are designed for the general public, students, academics, journalists, policymakers, and anyone interested in understanding how modern economies function.

  • Are your resources accessible to beginners?

    Yes. We create educational materials that break down complex economic concepts into clear, understandable language for all experience levels.

  • How can I get involved or stay updated?

    You can join our events, follow us on social media, or subscribe to our newsletter for updates on new resources and upcoming activities.
